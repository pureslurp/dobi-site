Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DoBi before you buy.

So DoBi is ChatGPT?

No. ChatGPT needs powerful cloud servers in massive data centers, huge compute resources, and constant internet. DoBi runs 100% locally on your regular computer with much simpler hardware. Since DoBi doesn’t have access to kilowatts of energy and giant high-compute models running in those data centers, its responses are more focused and direct (specialized in survival knowledge) rather than as fast, long, or broadly creative as cloud AIs. But they’re always available, completely private, and grid-independent. Perfect when it actually counts.

What's the difference between DoBi OS, DoBi, and DoBi Nitro?

DoBi OS is a ~25 GB downloadable ZIP file containing the full DoBi software. Just download, extract, and click to run. DoBi is our most popular option: a 1 TB portable SSD with everything pre-loaded, shipped straight to your door. Plug it in and go. DoBi Nitro is a custom-built mini computer with DoBi pre-installed, built to order for users who want a dedicated, always-ready DoBi machine. All three editions include the same DoBi software, AI models, and survival knowledge base.

Does DoBi only know about survival?

No, DoBi knows about a wide range of topics. It's built on a powerful pre-trained AI model that already understands a wide range of topics. Although DoBi will generally try to help you with survival topics, you can ask it about anything you want.

What hardware do I need?

Any reasonably modern computer running macOS or Linux. For Mac, that's a MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, iMac, or Mac Mini from roughly 2019 or newer. For Linux, most desktops and laptops will work. DoBi needs at least 4 GB of RAM (8 GB or more is recommended for the best experience). It auto-detects your hardware and picks the best model for your machine. If you have a newer Mac or an NVIDIA graphics card, DoBi will use GPU acceleration automatically for faster responses.

What topics does DoBi cover?

DoBi is built on a powerful pre-trained AI model that already understands a wide range of topics. On top of that, it ships with over 20,000 pages of expert survival material spanning water purification, emergency medicine, first aid, foraging and wild plants, food preservation and canning, gardening, hunting and trapping, shelter construction, wilderness survival, navigation, military field operations, knots and ropework, nuclear preparedness, and much more. You can also upload your own PDFs to expand DoBi's knowledge with any reference material you choose.

What AI models are included?

Three tiers are included with every edition of DoBi: Full, Mid, and Lite. DoBi automatically selects the best one for your computer, but you can switch between them at any time. The Full model gives the most detailed answers and works best on newer or more powerful machines. The Lite model is lighter and runs well even on older laptops with limited memory.

It depends on which edition you choose. DoBi OS: you'll receive an instant download link for a ZIP file after purchase. Extract it and click to launch. You'll be up in about a minute. DoBi (SSD): you'll receive a high-quality 1 TB portable USB-C SSD with everything pre-loaded. We source from trusted brands like Crucial, Samsung, Kingston, Lexar, ADATA, or SanDisk, always USB 3.2 Gen 2 or better. Each unit is hand-prepared and ships within 5–7 business days from Michigan, USA. DoBi Nitro: you'll receive a fully configured mini PC, custom-built to your specifications. Contact us for details.

Can I use the SSD on more than one machine?

Yes, as long as the machines are the same platform (both Mac or both Linux). Just unplug and move it to another machine. DoBi is portable by design.

Can I add my own PDFs to the knowledge base?

Yes. DoBi has a built-in Knowledge Base section in the sidebar where you can drag-and-drop or click to upload any text-based PDF. Your uploaded files are automatically indexed and become part of DoBi's knowledge base immediately. No restart or rebuild needed. Add purchased survival manuals, medical references, field guides, homesteading books, or any other PDF reference material you want DoBi to know about. You can view and remove uploaded files at any time. All processing happens locally. Your files never leave your machine. Note that DoBi needs text-based PDFs; scanned image-only PDFs without OCR text won't be usable.

Is my data private?

Completely. DoBi never connects to the internet. Your questions, conversations, and data never leave your machine. There's no account, no sign-up, and no tracking of any kind.
